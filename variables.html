Variables

In programming, variables are used to hold values. These values are saved until you change them.

city = "Bremerton"
In this example, city is the variable name, and "Bremerton" is the value.

year = 2016
In this example, year is the variable name, and 2016 is the value. Here, 2016 is a number, not a String. You can use variables in everything that you've already learned, in both printing text and doing Math operations.

Change the value of the name and age variables to match your own name and age.
