Text, or words in computer science are called Strings.

You can create a String by simply surrounding your text with quotes, either double-quotes (" ") or single-quotes(' '), just make sure to stick with one type.

You can even add Strings together by using the addition sign:
"Happy " + "Birthday"
Be sure to add your own spaces between your words because String addition does not do this automatically.

After running the program, try to print two more Strings of your own, using String addition for one of them.
